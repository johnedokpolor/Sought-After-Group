b. Human Resources System Setup & Capacity Building
We support organizations in building the foundation of a structured, functional, and sustainable Human Resources operation.
Many growing organizations operate for years without a formal HR system. Over time, this can lead to unclear processes, inconsistent practices, and leadership carrying responsibilities that should be supported by structured HR functions.
At Sought After Group, we work alongside organizations to deskkign and establish the systems needed for an effective HR function.
Our approach is flexible and tailored to your organization’s needs. This may include:
Structuring HR responsibilities within your existing team (e.g., equipping an administrative staff member to take on HR functions) 
Supporting the transition and setup of a dedicated internal HR role 
Providing an assigned HR resource from our team to manage both HR and administrative responsibilities
